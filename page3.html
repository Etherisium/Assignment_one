Honkai Star Rail

Honkai Star Rail is a mobile role-playing game set in the expansive universe of "Honkai Impact." Players embark on a journey through time and space, assembling a team of valiant warriors known as "Starlits" to combat the Honkai menace and restore balance to the fractured world.

My Favourite Character

Danheng is a male character in "Honkai Star Rail," recognized for his resolute determination and enigmatic background. As a formidable warrior, he employs a blend of technological prowess and magical abilities to combat the Honkai menace. His journey intertwines with others, unveiling mysteries and shaping the destiny of the universe.
