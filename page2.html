Tears of Themis

Tears of Themis is a romance mystery visual novel game, blending captivating storytelling with investigative gameplay. Set in a modern urban world, players assume the role of a young lawyer, solving intriguing cases while navigating complex relationships. It offers immersive experiences filled with drama, suspense, and romance."

My Favourite Character

Artem Wing is a character in "Tears of Themis," known for his enigmatic persona and exceptional intellect. As a member of the prestigious law firm Themis, he possesses keen analytical skills and a sharp wit, often assisting the protagonist in unraveling complex legal mysteries with his strategic insights.
